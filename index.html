my favorite artist of all time.

created by marian hernandez

As an avid music enjoyer, I listen to a lot of different genres. I listen to almost anything (except for country music, I tried to give it a chance), whether it be nostalgic 2000s hits, k-pop, j-pop, hispanic music, or r&b to name a few. This also means that I've had my few shares of hyperfixations in different artists and their music. However, as of recent, I've been absolutely obsessing over someone I've been actually listening to for years. I just haven't really taken the time to really obsess over her. Her recent songs that have been released really pulled me back into the rabbit hole I dug for myself back in 2017.

Growing up, I've always watched Disney Channel and Nickelodeon and emersed myself in all of the hilarious (at the time) shows. I could watch them for hours on end. One was "Girl Meets World", to which I didn't show much interest to. The only thing I really took interest to was Sabrina Carpenter because I thought she was GORGEOUS, and when I found out that she made music? I was sat.

So, without further ado. Here are my top three favorite Sabrina Carpenter songs ranking from third to first!

because i liked a boy

too iconic not to mention.

because i liked a boy is a song that resonates with me so much. As corny as it sounds, this song helped me get through several -- and I mean several heartbreaks. The lyrics hit a bit too close to home, giving me some sort of exposure therapy, and that combined with Ms. Carpenter's angelic voice actually helped so much. I can fully remember myself listening to this song in the car, in the shower, right before I go to bed, while I do my makeup. People might call me basic for putting this song at third place, but it's truly a song that has a special place in my heart.

don't smile

fair warning, the next two songs are from the short n' sweet album, sorry not sorry.

This song from Sabrina's album is not talked about enough. Don't Smile has such an ethereal vibe to it, and the adlibs add so much to it. As a vocal major myself, I can't help but listen to the additional melodeies and harmonies that are solely Sabrina's voice. This song in specific has a playful but sad twist on the casual saying, "Don't cry because it's over, smile because it happened," to which Sabrina says the opposite. Sabrina has a way of playing with words and making such playful and unique lyrics that make almost anyone know at least one of them. Even my own father knows a good handful of songs from Short n' Sweet.

slim pickins

this is extremely ironic, this is a country-sounding song. whoopsie!

slim. freaking. pickins. I don't even know what the title even means, and it's my favorite song off the album EASILY. Sabrina sang it before Short n' Sweet came out at a little performance she did, deciding to tease one of the song on the album for her awaiting fans. I remember seeing a video of her singing it on stage before the album came out, and I was gagged to say the least. When I heard it on the album on Spotify, I kid you not this woman sounded exactly how she did in the video I saw. Miss ma'am does NAWT need no autotune, and her tour videos are there as PROOF. (I'll forever be upset that I didn't go and see her, she was literally TEN MINUTES FROM MY HOUSE.) This is extremely ironic and a mental slap to the face because I said that I don't partuclarly like country music in the introduction to this project, but if it's Miss Carpenter I can manage it :) her voice just goes so well with that genre of music.
